# Monetization (V1)

Entrestate V1 is sold as **products** (self‑serve) plus **services** (managed execution).

## 1) Products (Self‑Serve)

### 1.1 Core Suite — $18 / month
Includes:

**Smart Builder**
- Build landing pages for any project.
- Templates + sections.
- Refiner checklist before publish (CTA, WhatsApp, contrast, hero, form quality).

**Chat Agent**
- Works on landing pages and website embed.
- Share link + QR code for bio.
- Captures name/phone/intent, pushes the conversation to WhatsApp, and saves the lead.
- Pause auto‑replies when you want to take over.

**Projects Inventory**
- Public inventory to show credibility and market depth.
- Embed inventory on your landing page or website.
- Logged‑in search gives deeper browsing and export options.

### 1.2 Market Data Download — $226 (one‑time)
- A one‑time purchase to download the dataset (customer chooses format).
- Positioned as “pay once, own forever”.
- Intended for analysts, portals, agencies, and internal BI.

## 2) Services (Managed)

### 2.1 Google Ads (run from our ad account)
- Broker does not need to create ad accounts or learn keywords.
- You fund a spend wallet, we run, monitor, and report.
- Success is defined upfront (budget, timeline, lead expectations, and assumptions).

Charging model (V1):
- Prepaid spend wallet (default).
- Service fee rules live in Billing (spend caps, pauses, invoice history).

## 3) PayPal (V1)
- PayPal subscription: Core Suite $18 / month.
- PayPal one‑time payment: Market Data Download $226.

## 4) Notes
- Keep product language action‑based. Avoid hype words: magic, generate, our.
- Use: smart, instant creation, refiner, publish, connect, launch.
